Purpose

This website is dedicated to promoting Chinese embroidery culture and showcasing the exquisite craftsmanship and artistic charm of the millennium heritage. We bring together the four famous embroidery styles (Suzhou embroidery, Hunan embroidery, Guangdong embroidery, and Sichuan embroidery), and provide a wealth of historical introductions, fine product appreciation, and modern application cases. Whether you are a handicraft enthusiast, designer, or cultural researcher, you can gain a deeper understanding of the unique charm of embroidery here and jointly promote the inheritance and innovation of this intangible cultural heritage.
